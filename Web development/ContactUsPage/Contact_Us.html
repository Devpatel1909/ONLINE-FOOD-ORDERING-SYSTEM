  • What are the mandatory documents needed to list my restaurant on Food Junction?

    • You will need your FSSAI license, GST certificate, PAN card, bank account details, and identity proof to list your restaurant on Food Junction.

  • After I submit all documents, how long will it take for my restaurant to go live on Food Junction?

    • It usually takes 7-10 business days after all documents are successfully verified.

  • What is this one-time Onboarding fee? Do I have to pay for it while registering?

    • Yes, there is a one-time onboarding fee to cover the cost of setting up your restaurant on Food Junction. Payment is made during registration.

  • Who should I contact if I need help & support in getting onboarded?

    • Contact us at support@foodjunction.com or reach out to our dedicated onboarding team through the help section on our website.

  • How much commission will I be charged by Food Junction?

    • The commission varies based on the type of restaurant and city. You will be informed about the applicable commission before onboarding.

  • I don’t have an FSSAI licence for my restaurant. Can it still be onboarded?

    • No, an FSSAI license is mandatory to onboard your restaurant on Food Junction.

  • What is Food Junction Customer Care Number?

    • We offer a chat-based support system for quick issue resolution. Simply search for your issue in the help section and initiate a chat. Email support: support@foodjunction.com

  • I want to explore career opportunities with Food Junction

    • Join our team by sending your resume to careers@foodjunction.com.

  • Can I edit my order?

    • Orders can be edited before they reach the restaurant. Once the restaurant starts preparing, editing is no longer possible.

  • Can I cancel my order?

    • You can cancel the order if it hasn't been placed at the restaurant. A cancellation fee may apply if the order is confirmed.

  • Do you charge for delivery?

    • Delivery fees vary by location and restaurant. You can see the applicable delivery fee on the 'Review Order' page.

  • Can I order in advance?

    • Currently, all orders on Food Junction are placed on-demand, and advance orders are not supported.

  • How long do you take to deliver?

    • Delivery times vary by location and the restaurant’s availability.

  • What are your delivery hours?

    • Delivery hours vary by location. Check your area to see available delivery times.

  • Can I change the delivery address/number after placing an order?

    • Minor changes (like flat number) are possible. Major changes in the address are not allowed once the order is placed.

  • Did not receive OTP?

    • Ensure your app is updated. If the issue persists, contact support@foodjunction.com.

  • Deactivate my account

    • Contact support@foodjunction.com to request account deactivation.
